ABOUT

wave to earth are a South Korean indie-rock band consisting of members Daniel (vocals), Dongkyu (drums), and John (guitar). They made their debut on August 23, 2019 with the single 'wave' followed by 'light' on November 18. They released their first EP 'wave 0.1' on January 2, 2020.
