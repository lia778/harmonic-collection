BACKGROUND

Underscores, otherwise known as Devon Karpf, is a hyperpop artist from San Francisco who got their start on SoundCloud. Fishmonger is their 2021 debut album which contains 10 tracks including "Your favorite sidekick".

ABOUT

"Your favorite sidekick" stands out to me particularly because of how well it combines a playful melody with visceral intrumentals. As a whole though, the song definitely embodies the aesthetics of angsty teenagers doing angsty teenager things. Throughout the track, you get the occasional punk guitar section but more importantly, a routine bass that is almost deafening. As surprising as it is at first, over time the bass became one of my favorite aspects of the song. It adds a sense of satisfaction everytime it plays after a verse.
